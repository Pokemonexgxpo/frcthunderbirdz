ABOUT THUNDERBIRDZ

Team 10107 Thunderbirdz is a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team based in Central Florida. We're a group of passionate high school students who design, build, and program competitive robots while learning valuable STEM and leadership skills.

Founded in 2018, our team has grown to over 25 dedicated members from different backgrounds, all united by our love for robotics and innovation.

OUR TEAM

The Thunderbirdz team is comprised of dedicated students who work together across different sub-teams:

  • Mechanical Team: Designs and builds the robot structure, mechanisms and systems
  • Electrical Team: Creates and maintains all electrical systems and wiring
  • Programming Team: Develops code for robot operation, autonomous modes, and vision systems
  • Business/Outreach Team: Manages sponsorships, community relations, and team branding
